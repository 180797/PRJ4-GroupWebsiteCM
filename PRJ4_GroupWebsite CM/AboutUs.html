WHO WE ARE

We are Clear Mountain, a young company that sells boxes. Yes, boxes. Filled with beautiful products, collected from all around the world, but also just from around the corner. We try to collect the most useful, simple, beautiful and environment friendly items we can get. We sort them out, see what fits together. OUR VISION

In a world of environmental dissatisfaction, we believe that everyone should live a zero waste lifestyle, because only then we can save the planet. We value innovation, initiative, commitment and growth. And hope we can spread those values a bit via the boxes, sharing innovation, appreciating initiative, committing to a better future together and growing in our more conscious lifestyles.

OUR MISION

We like to make living more conscious and environment friendly as easy as possible. By bringing all handy products to one place, informing you about how to use them, tips and tricks and we hope to bring you a smile on your face. You can order different boxes for yourself, our spread the happiness and give a box to a friend. So that we can all see how easy it can be and how good it feels to help with the little things.
