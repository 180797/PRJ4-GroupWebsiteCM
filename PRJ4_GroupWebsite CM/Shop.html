The General Box

The General Box contains an arrangement of random sustainable goodies that will help you with living in an easy and sustainable way. The products are mostly home, garden and kitchen tools. This box is perfect to give to that friend that could live a little more sustainable or just for your self to make the earth a little happier and contribute to keeping the earth clean.


The Beauty Box

The Beauty Box contains a wide arrangement of beautiful sustainable beauty products like hand cream, face masks, tonics and more. This is the perfect box to treat yourself, your girlfriend and ofcourse the sustainable geeks. The Box comes with a description of every product and tells the stories of the products.


The Granola Box

The Granola Box contains a variety of different kinds of sustainable produced granola and granola bars, recipes and some tools to easily make it yourself. This box will help you with having a sustainable breakfast, lunch or snack. Perfect for the granola lover or the health geeks.
